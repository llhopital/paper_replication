To replicate the results of "No Quick Fix: The Recovery and Resilience Plan and External Position in Greece" by Aligishiev and Blotevogel (2025):

1. Download and extract this replication package to your local machine.

2. Run the script replicate_results.m to reproduce the main quantitative results.

Figures 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 and all figures in the appendices of the paper will be automatically generated and saved in the ./charts subfolder. 

Requirements: MATLAB must be installed. The package includes Dynare 4.5.6.
